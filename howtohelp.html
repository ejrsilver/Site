Amnesty Program

If your playing days are behind you or you know where we might find some bagpipes, drums, etc., we are always looking for instruments for our new players. We have skilled instructors who can give your old sets some TLC and they might be greatly loved by a future piper. All of our lessons are free, and we try to have equipment (including uniforms and instruments) to loan to anyone who needs it. Your donation could help a young person become a piper!

Community Partner

We are always looking for ways to be involved in the community, particularly with young people to help them start on their lifelong involvement in pipe and drum bands.
The Kingston Police Pipe Band currently has an active partnership with the Boys and Girls Club of Kingston. We share space with them for our practices, and band members volunteer in the after-school program providing pipe and drum lessons.
Do you have a partnership idea? contact us at info@kingstonpolicepipeband.com.
