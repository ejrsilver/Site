Take Lessons

Classes are available for new beginners to advanced students starting at age 8 and up. All equipment provided. Click here to learn more. [hyperlink to ‘learn to play’] Contact us at kingstonpolicepipeband@gmail.com to register.

Join the band

If you are a beginner, learn more here [hyperlink to Learn to play]. If you have experience, come out to a practice on Sunday evening and introduce yourself.

Time: Sundays at 6:30pm to 8:30pm
Location: Robert Meek on Bagot st.

Contact Pipe Major John McKay at kingstonpolicepipeband@gmail.com and let him know you are coming.

Volunteer

Along with playing with band as a volunteer, we also have fundraising events and promotional events. We can use folks to do all kinds of jobs. Contact the band at kingstonpolicepipeband@gmail.com to find out about current volunteer opportunities.

Examples include:

  • Working booths for fundraising or promotional events
  • Bingo
  • Moving instruments
  • Carrying our banner in the parade
  • Setting up and take down
  • Anything you can think of
