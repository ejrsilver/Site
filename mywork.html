Free Lessons

Time: Sundays at 5:30pm to 6:30pm

Location: Robert Meek on Bagot st.

Classes are available for new beginners to advanced students starting at age 8 and up. Students will be loaned practice equipment at no cost while they continue their free lessons with our organization. The lessons are usually 45 min to an hour in length.

Our teaching program is taught by qualified members of the Kingston Police Pipe Band who are sworn members of the Kingston Police Community Volunteers. The instructors are experienced players and competitors that have proven to be proficient at a high level.

Our students work towards accredited music certifications, performances, and competitions.

Music Certification

We offer our students the opportunity to earn accredited music certifications in Scottish Bagpipes, Scottish Pipe Band Drumming, and in teaching these instruments. Each year Assessors from the Piping and Drumming Qualifications Board (PDQB) in Scotland are brought in to conduct the examinations. Certificates in piping and drumming at all levels involve both Theory and Performance (practical) examinations.
